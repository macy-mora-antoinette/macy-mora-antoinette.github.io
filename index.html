About Me

With a dynamic blend of advanced data analysis, machine learning expertise, and a proven track record in cutting-edge research, I am a seasoned data scientist eager to drive impactful solutions for your team. My extensive experience at Cornell University, MIT and Twitter, coupled with proficiency in Python, R, and MATLAB, has equipped me to tackle complex data challenges. My PhD research at Cornell University combines imaging, neurotechnology development, and orthopedic mechanical systems. I am very fortunate to have my research funded by fellowships from the Ford Foundation (National Academy of Sciences, Medicine, and Engineering) and the Mong Family Foundation (Cornell Neurotech). Prior to that, I completed my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UC San Diego. I continued my education with a M.S. in Computational Data Analytics from Georgia Tech. I have successfully leveraged my skills to decipher nuanced data patterns and develop actionable insights. Whether it's unraveling biological mysteries or enhancing business decisions, I am committed to applying data-driven methodologies to achieve excellence.
